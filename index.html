About Me

I am a PhD Student at Imperial College London in the Large-scale Data and Systems (LSDS) group, and a member of the Imperial College Centre for Cryptocurrency Research and Engineering (IC3RE). I'm also the primary author and lead for the Teechain Network, a secure high-performance off-chain payment network for blockchains.

Under Prof. Peter Pietzuch my research focuses on improving the security and privacy of large-scale distributed systems. Most recently, I have focused on improving the security of cloud infrastructures and peer-to-peer networks, such as blockchains and cryptocurrencies, through the use of trusted hardware.

Feel free to contact me at ku.ca.lairepmi@11dnil.auhsoj if you would like to chat or if you would like an up-to-date copy of my CV.
