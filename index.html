About Me

I am a second year PhD Student at Imperial College London in the Large-scale Distributed Systems (LSDS) group. Under Prof. Peter Pietzuch my research focuses on improving the security and privacy of large-scale distributed systems. Most recently, I have focused on improving the security of cloud infrastructures and peer-to-peer networks, such as blockchains and cryptocurrencies, through the use of trusted hardware. More generally, I find all areas of systems security fascinating. Before starting my PhD I received a Masters of Engineering (MEng) in Computing from Imperial College London.

Feel free to contact me at ku.ca.lairepmi@11dnil.auhsoj if you would like to chat or if you would like an up-to-date copy of my CV.
